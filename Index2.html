Who am I?

My name is Samarth Ashwathanarayana. I am a student pursuing the Bachelor of Design programme in PES University. I am an enthusiastic, friendly chap who enjoys the outdoors and meeting knew people. I love being a flautist, and playing Basketball. Most importantly, I love my work- The idea of giving birth to something new, be it for solving a problem or for self-expression, is exhilarating to me. Creativity is the language of a free mind, and the Design field is teaching me to speak it fluently.
